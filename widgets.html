Welcome To The Halls Of Residence

Our halls offer a diverse and vibrant living environment, providing students with comfortable accommodation and a sense of community. Each hall has its own unique character and facilities to ensure a pleasant and enriching living experience. Explore our halls below to find out more.

Male Halls

Makerere University offers a diverse range of accommodation options with a total of six male halls, each providing a unique living experience. These halls are designed to cater to the various needs and preferences of our male students, ensuring a comfortable and supportive environment for academic and personal growth. Our male halls include:Nkrumah,Lumumba,Nsibirwa,Mitchell,Livingstone,University Hall

Female Halls

At Makerere University, we take pride in offering a distinguished living experience for our female students through three prestigious halls. These halls are dedicated to empowering and nurturing ladies of substance, providing an environment that fosters academic excellence, personal growth, and leadership. These Halls are MaryStuart,Complex,Africa
