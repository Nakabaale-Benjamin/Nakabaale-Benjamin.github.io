Welcome to the Hall Application Portal!

Our platform is designed to streamline the application process for students seeking accommodation in university halls. Whether you are a new applicant or a returning resident, our system ensures a seamless experience from application submission to room assignment. Stay informed about your application status and receive notifications directly through your profile. Start your journey with us today and secure your place in the hall!

How to Apply

To apply for accommodation in the university halls, students must use their official university email addresses and registration numbers. Please ensure that you have both of these details ready before starting the application process. Begin by logging into the Hall Application Portal using your university email. Follow the prompts to enter your registration number and complete the application form. Accurate information is crucial, so double-check all entries before submission. This will ensure a smooth and efficient application process, and help us to serve you better.
